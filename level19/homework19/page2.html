Statue of Zeus at Olympia

The monumental statue of zeus at olympia in greece was one of the seven wonders of the ancient world

this huge statue was made of ivory and gold Worshipped by pilgrims from across the Mediterranean, the statue inspired countless imitations and defined the standard representation of Zeus in Greek and Roman art in sculpture, on coins, pottery, and gemstones.

this masterpiece captivated the ancient world for 1,000 years and was the must-see sight for anyone who attended the ancient Olympic Games.
